2022/2023 Undergraduate Admission Application Portal

This is to inform all UTME and Direct Entry candidates who applied for admission into the Jayden Owen Xavion University(JOX), Lagos for the 2022/2023 academic session that registration for Admissions Screening Exercise has commenced.

Candidates who made JOX UNIVERISTY their first choice of institution and scored 200 and above in the 2021 and 2022 UTME are eligible for POST UTME Screening Exercise. In addition, candidates who choose the University as second choice or did not choose it at all are advised to do a change of institution to JOX UNIVERSITY as first choice on JAMB website and Direct Entry candidates should also choose JOX UNIVERSITY as their first choice before registering. Candidates must possess five (5) ‘0’ Level credit passes in relevant subjects including Mathematics and English Language at not more than two (2) sittings.

Eligibility

• 5 (Five) O'Level Credits

Guidelines To Candidates For On-Line Registration

i. Candidates should use a Username to Create Account and make payment on this Platform.

ii. Candidates are to obtain a print-out containing the Transaction ID upon payment of ₦20,000.00 only.

iii. After successful payment, candidates are automatically redirected to the Application page to complete their Data Form online.
